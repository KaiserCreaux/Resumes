Services

I begin with a no-cost 15 minute phone consultation, followed by a meeting.

  • For $50, I'll work with you over videochat, telephone or email.
  • For $75, we can meet in person at an NYC-area coffeehouse of your choice.
  • For $100, I'll come to your NYC-area office.

I will take your resume as it stands or help you build one that brings out all you have to offer!

I also help with cover letters, either in addition to a resume or as a stand-alone service. Adding a cover letter is $35, and a cover letter as a stand-alone is the price of the above appointments.

There is also an additional $35 charge for a secondary resume (for a different industry, as an example).

10% discount for students, military/veterans or those who have been out of work for more than 3 months.

I cannot promise a job or an interview, but I can promise a fresh look at where your career is heading and how to get there, and an excellent resume to take you there.
